    Plant Managers

  • What Problem It Solves

    Top Flow organizes internal logistics by improving part accessibility, ensuring FIFO flow, reducing handling time, and increasing visual control across the facility.

Top Flow is a modular logistics system composed of structures, rails, buggies, and accessories that work together to ensure smooth and efficient intra-facility material flow.

Core Business

  • Shopstocks
    Modular accumulation structures that optimize space and ensure long-lasting durability.
  • Structures & Rails
    Sturdy, reconfigurable structures and rails that support smooth and continuous part movement.
  • Buggies
    Mobile carriers that transport and accumulate parts safely, allowing single or multiple buggy connections.
  • Transfer bars
    Aerial rails that enable smooth material transfer between work areas while keeping aisles clear.
  • Track Switches & Curves
    Directional elements that allow flexible routing, ergonomic positioning, and true FIFO flow.
  • Multi-link buggy
    Durable multi-link buggies for large or multiple parts, featuring reinforced welded joints and metallic inserts for high impact resistance.

Advanced Capabilities

  • Gravity-Assisted Flow
    Gravity-assisted configurations that support smoother part movement between stations.
  • Multilevel & Mezzanines
    Multilevel systems and mezzanines designed to maximize vertical space and accumulation capacity.
  • Working Stations
    Ergonomic workstations that bring parts closer to operators, improving safety and productivity.

Part Handling Solutions

  • Metal hangers (hooks)
    Custom-designed metal hangers developed to safely handle a wide variety of automotive parts.
  • Bags & Textile Separators
    In-house manufactured textile solutions that ensure proper nesting and part protection.

Engineering, Layouts & Implementation

Our engineering team analyzes each facility to design optimized layouts and implement solutions tailored to production and logistics requirements.

Innovation & Patents

Top Flow is protected by multiple industrial and utility model patents, reflecting continuous innovation and engineering expertise.
